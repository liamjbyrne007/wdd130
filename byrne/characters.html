Example Characters

Here you can read about some characters and their stories to help inspire what you can do with your own character.

Bhorgar Battlehammer

Bhorgar is a relatively young dwarf who is seeking adventure so he may prove himself worthy of his family’s legacy, a priceless set of armor and weapons. Contrary to common dwarvish tendency, he prefers to fight from range, often throwing his axes.

Greg

Greg is an ancient dwarf wizard, whose age is lost to time. He started adventuring because retirement was rather dull for him. Since setting off on the road, he’s had a much more exciting time, and he’s recently upset the power balance of the local goblin tribes after deposing their king.

Elder Joeferd

Elder Joeferd is a peculiar halfling who loves to think he’s in charge of everything. When he says everything like he is, people do what he says too. He’s also an excellent chef.
